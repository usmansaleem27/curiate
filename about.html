Who We Are

The numbers don't lie. In 2024, around 25-30% of online fashion purchases, worth $275 billion, will be returned, many ending up in landfills. An estimated 9.5 million tons of apparel waste filled 2,950 football fields in the U.S. alone. Without innovative solutions, this number will grow. The future of waste and returns relies on efficient and advanced technology integration to curb this crisis and reduce environmental waste in the apparel and fashion industry.
